Bureaus & Divisions

The Los Santos Police Department is organized into several highly specialized divisions to ensure effective law enforcement and community service.

The Backbone of the Department

Patrol Division

The Patrol Division is the largest and most visible component of the LSPD. Officers in this division are the first responders to emergency 911 calls, traffic accidents, and community disturbances. They are dedicated to proactive policing, community engagement, and maintaining a safe environment across all districts of Los Santos.

  • 24/7 City-wide Coverage
  • Community Oriented Policing
  • First Response & Crisis Management
Tactical Response

Metropolitan Division (S.W.A.T)

The Metropolitan Division handles high-risk situations that exceed the capabilities of standard patrol officers. This includes hostage rescues, heavy barricade situations, counter-terrorism operations, and high-risk warrant serving. Operators undergo rigorous physical and psychological training.
