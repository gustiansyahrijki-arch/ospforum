Our Core Values

Integrity

We are committed to the highest standards of moral and ethical conduct. We hold ourselves accountable for our actions and take pride in our professional level of service.

Respect

We treat all people with dignity, fairness, and compassion. We respect the diverse cultural backgrounds of the Los Santos community.

Courage

We stand ready to face danger and adversity to protect others. We have the mental and moral strength to do what is right, even in the face of opposition.

Our Jurisdiction

The Los Santos Police Department is responsible for policing the city limits of Los Santos, San Andreas. From the bustling streets of Downtown to the quiet neighborhoods of Vinewood and the industrial sectors of Cypress Flats, our officers are deployed strategically to ensure rapid response times and effective community coverage.
