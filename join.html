Do you have what it takes to protect Los Santos?

Basic Requirements

  • • Must be at least 21 years of age.
  • • High school diploma or GED equivalent.
  • • Valid San Andreas Driver's License.
  • • No prior felony convictions.
  • • Excellent physical and mental condition.

The Hiring Process

  1. Submit Online Application
  2. Written Examination
  3. Physical Agility Test (PAT)
  4. Background Investigation
  5. Psychological & Medical Evaluation
  6. Police Academy Training
